Anil Ballal


"Life is from birth to death, but living is only in the present. Make it count. Being Self-aware, attentive, productive." - Anil Ballal


Anil Ballal is a teacher of Vedanta, the sublime philosophy of ancient India. His selfless contribution attunes its philosophical themes to a practical technique of action. An exercise that has brought about mental solace and material comfort in society.

He has a degree in Electrical Engineering, worked in industry, and also studied Vedanta. On gaining Its Knowledge, he renounced his family business and rising career, to research. And with a global Self-aware vision, has dedicated his life to the study, research and propagation of Vedanta. By shedding Its Light through poems, books, lectures and discourses.


At 60, he has a 4am to 9pm spiritually disciplined routine of yoga - karma, bhakti, and jnana. Attending essential duties without needing weekends off, vacations, entertainment or sightseeing. Ideal in weight, blood pressure and sugar levels. Healthy, having given away luxuries, and dedicated himself to a life of service and sacrifice.
