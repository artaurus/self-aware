Anil Ballal


"Life is from birth to death but living is only in the present. Make it count. Being Self-aware, attentive and productive, for Enlightenment." - Anil Ballal


Kernel. Anil Ballal is a philosopher and teacher of Vedanta, the sublime philosophy of ancient India. His selfless contribution in 23 years of research attunes its philosophy to practical action. An exercise that has brought about mental relief and material comfort in society.


Light. Anil graduated in Electrical Engineering, and while working in industry, also studied, reflected upon and taught Vedanta. Shifting from outer to inner spiritual engineering, he gained its Knowledge. Then renounced his family business and rising career midway to research it.

Service. Seeing its importance and practicality, Anil has dedicated his life to the study, research and propagation of Vedanta. Teaching his students and sharing its Knowledge globally through books, lectures and talks.
