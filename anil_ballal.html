Anil Ballal


Anil Ballal is a philosopher and exponent of Vedanta, the sublime philosophy of ancient India. He attunes its Core theme, Knowledge of your Real Self, to higher values and a technique of living. Which has enabled unstressed peace, productivity and prosperity in society.


He has a degree in Electrical Engineering, and worked in industry including family business. During which, inspired by Swami Parthasarthy, and guided by his Guru, he also studied and reflected Vedanta, imparted it through classes, and discovered its Knowledge. Then he renounced business to research Vedanta.


With a global vision and 22 years of ongoing spiritual discipline, he has dedicated his life to the study, research and propagation of Vedanta. He is selflessly conveying It through writings, discourses and seminars.
