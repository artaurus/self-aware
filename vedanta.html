Vedanta


Veda means Knowledge, and anta means end. Vedanta, literally meaning End of Knowledge, is the sublime philosophy of ancient India. It gives the purpose and principles of life and living, enables one to think original, choose the right course of action, and act on it. And above all, it leads to the Knowledge of your Real Self.


Timeless, Vedanta is eternal; it always was, is, and ever will be. Limitless, Vedanta is universal; in every star, rock, brook, tree, animal, and human being. Flawless, Vedanta stands on its own Truth; beyond race, creed, religion, and authority.


Individuals unnecessarily experience negatives like stress or ailments. Society and nations too experience collective negatives. So, along with growth and fleeting joys, life also offers challenges, mental dis-ease and physical decay, besides inevitable death. No lasting peace and happiness.


Practical Vedanta develops the intellect and purifies the mind through a spiritual discipline. Its higher values and techniques uplift above harmful negatives to divine thought, chaste emotions, selfless actions, and a clean environment. Harmoniously enhancing 3Ps in society - peace, productivity, prosperity.


And the philosophy of Vedanta develops spiritual Awareness of one's Real Self, which leads to everlasting peace and bliss in Self-realization, God-realization. Oneness with all. Your Real Self.
