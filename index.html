The Philosophy

Ved-anta means End of Knowledge. With a spiritual discipline in place, it develops Awareness of one's real Self. Being in this vast universal space of God-consciousness, i.e. Self-Awareness, it fortifies practical Harmonious Living. Even as its sublime philosophy leads to Self-realisation.

The Teacher

As a philosopher, Anil Ballal attunes Awareness of one's real Self in practical harmonious living, founded on Vedanta. His teachings illumine Self-Awareness, enhance Oneness, peacefully rest the mind, and enable productively helpful silence, words and deeds. It also leads to liberation in God-realisation, Self-realisation.
