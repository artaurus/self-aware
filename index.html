About Vedanta

Ved-anta literally means End of Knowledge. It explains the purpose of existence and eternal principles of life and living based on reason and logic. It enables one to think independently, neither with blind faith nor relying on authorities, and choose the right course of action.


Timeless, Vedanta is eternal; it always was, is, and will be. Limitless, Vedanta is universal; in every star, rock, brook, tree, animal, and human being. Beyond race, creed, religion, and authority, Vedanta stands on its own Truth.


Practical Vedanta develops the intellect and purifies the mind through a spiritual discipline. Reducing desires, it shines divine thought and chaste emotions behind productive actions. Which, performed selflessly on essential duties, yields both peace and prosperity.


And its sublime philosophy leads to God-realization, Self-realization. Your real Self.
