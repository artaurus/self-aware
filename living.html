Programmed living


  1. Take on a study and vocation to suit your svadharma, own nature, liking, natural inclination.

  2. Fix a goal in its field of activity, a higher ideal beyond self-centered interests, i.e, a common good worldly goal, or the all-inclusive spiritual goal, Self-realization. But not for achieving. Your goal is only for one role - it gives direction to your action in the present.

  3. Chalk a course of action towards achieving your goal. Adapt to suit present conditions. Include gaining the Knowledge of Self, higher values, and the technique of living, with unwavering faith and purifying devotion.

  4. Self-develop with the spiritual disciplines of karma yoga, bhakti yoga, and jnana yoga for union with your chosen goal.

  5. Clip thoughts of the past and future. Here and now, produce and give essential words and deeds. Self-aware, attentive, productive.
