Teaching for whom


If you are fulfilled, peaceful and happy, knowing that you are exactly the way you should be, then you can continue to be as you are, doing whatever it is that you do. You don't require any teaching.


But if you are not fulfilled, peaceful and happy here and now, as you experience harmful negatives like stress or aliments, and are affected by them, then this teaching can help you.


Or if you believe you are just another individual in the world, not the Real Self, and would like to realize that Self, then this teaching can help you.



The Dis-ease


Ignorance of your Real Self; the universal, ever-full, peaceful, and blissful Self.


Consequently, this brings up thoughts and desires, and actions to fulfill them. And you experience a few elevating joys but more of harmful negatives, and suffer. Dis-ease by disease.



The Cure


Vedanta. Gain the Knowledge of your Real Self, higher values and techniques of living. Being Self-aware then, perform actions in obligatory duties for a higher cause.


Consequently, desires and their harmful negatives reduce and end, while helpful positives rise and enhance the quality of life's experiences. It also leads to Self-realization, God-realization. The permanent cure.
