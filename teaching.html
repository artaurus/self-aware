The Dis-ease


Ignorance of your Real Self; the universal, ever-full, peaceful, and blissful Self.


Consequently, this brings up thoughts, desires and emotions, and actions to fulfill them. By which you do experience a few elevating joys, but more of harmful negatives like agitations, stress, and strain. Dis-ease by the disease of ignorance.



The Cure


Self-develop with the three spiritual disciplines - karma yoga, bhakti yoga, and jnana yoga for union with goal Self-realization - Self-aware then in duties, give words and deeds essential to goal.


Consequently, desires and their harmful negatives reduce, while helpful positives like a charitable attitude and noble emotions rise. Enhancing the quality of the unending stream of experiences.


  • Being Self-aware, youth can design, study and work on a thought-out career and other obligatory duties.

  • Being Self-aware, those who tend to get stressed, like professionals and businesspersons, can steer towards mental peace despite intense action.

  • Being Self-aware, a sincere and devoted seeker can attain Self-realization, God-realization, and reach the Ultimate State, Enlightenment. The permanent cure.


Think ...


Activities, actions and thoughts are forces of your choice. They consume energy. To use them optimally, productively, clip non-essential activities and thoughts. And act in the present on an essential task in hand.


Actions, perceptions, feelings and thoughts of the mind don't agitate, stress or strain you. Identifying with the mind does.


For peace and happiness, develop the intellect and use it to govern the mind, and also to shift perception from mind to Self.


Your Real Self is Brahman, Consciousness. Ever-full, peaceful, blissful. Identify with that Self through essential actions, perceptions, feelings and thoughts. Thereby being Self-aware, attentive, productive.


Perceive, and remember the Self.
