Teaching for whom


If you are fulfilled, peaceful and happy, knowing that you are exactly the way you should be, then you can continue to be as you are, doing whatever it is that you do. You don't require any teaching.


But if you are not fulfilled, peaceful and happy here and now, as you experience harmful negatives like stress or ailments, and are affected by them, then this teaching can help you.


Or if you believe you are just another individual in the world, not the Real Self, and would like to realize that Self, then also this teaching can help you.



The Dis-ease


Ignorance of your Real Self; the universal, ever-full, peaceful, and blissful Self.


Consequently, this brings up thoughts and desires, and actions to fulfill them. And you experience a few elevating joys but more of harmful negatives, and suffer. Dis-ease by the disease of ignorance.



The Cure


Vedanta. Learn the technique of living tuned to the Knowledge of your Real Self. Being Self-aware then, act on essentials to your chosen higher cause.


Consequently, desires and their harmful negatives reduce, while helpful positive thoughts and noble emotions rise. Enhancing the quality of life's experiences. And, if the higher cause is Self-realization, the Knowledge of Self also leads seekers to the Ultimate State, Enlightenment. The permanent cure.
